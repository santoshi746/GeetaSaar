Welcome to GeetaSaar Foundation, where we don’t just talk about change; we make it happen! Our mission is to empower the incredible individuals and provide right path through education and learning. We together shall strive hard to live and achieve that, every girl shine, and we are here to make that happen!

What is Education:

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes through learning, experience, or teaching. It involves the development of intellectual, emotional, and social abilities that help individuals understand the world, make informed decisions, and contribute to society. Education can take place in formal settings like schools and universities, or through informal learning experiences throughout life.

Education through GeetsSaar:

Through GeetaSaar, we will be over coming academic gaps and encourage holistic development, and foresee long-term transformation. With a vision of self-sufficiency and economic empowerment, we aspire to create a future in which information, skills, and confidence drive success, resulting in a better tomorrow for both individuals and communities.

Our Offerings

We are aimed to start with teaching the youth in excelling their academics, guiding and mentoring them, and also aspire them to professional courses. Not only academics for the class 11th , 12th and Under Graduation, we also shall aspire them to choose professional courses and also take a path to be a CA, CS or CWA. We’ve got them all. Let’s turn those dreams into reality!
