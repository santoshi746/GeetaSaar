About us

GeetaSaar is a not-for-profit organization, formed with a sole intention to make education accessible to every individual, and reach every corner including villages. GeetaSaar is not just as a place of education for today, but is an Eternal Pillar dedicated to provide accessible education to generations and generations.

We believe that every soul has some inherent and valuable potentiality. GeetaSaar is aimed to identify that potentiality, unlock the same and make lives meaningful.

This foundation is named as GeetaSaar, which name has been inspired by the book “Bhagvad Geeta”, the most scared scriptures of Hinduism, which teaches the way to lead one’s own life with clarity, peace and achieve the self-mastery. The book Bhagavad Geeta addresses the human condition, exploring the meaning of life, duty (Dharma), the nature of the self, and the path to liberation.
