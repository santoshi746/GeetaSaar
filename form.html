About us

  • GeetaSaar is a not-for-profit organization, formed with a sole intention to make education accessible to every individual, and reach every corner including villages.
  • GeetaSaar is not just as a place of education for today, but is an Eternal Pillar dedicated to provide accessible education to generations and generations. We believe that every soul has some inherent and valuable potentiality.
  • GeetaSaar is aimed to identify that potentiality, unlock the same and make lives meaningful.
  • This foundation is named as GeetaSaar, which name has been inspired by the book “Bhagvad Geeta”, the most scared scriptures of Hinduism, which teaches the way to lead one’s own life with clarity, peace and achieve the self-mastery.
  • The book Bhagavad Geeta addresses the human condition, exploring the meaning of life, duty (Dharma), the nature of the self, and the path to liberation.

"To bridge the vast gap in education system, empower dreams, transform lives and create a brighter future for youth, particularly girls, women, rural communities and establisj a self-reliance model."

GOAL

"To reach every village in Rajasthan and ensure that every individual has the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed"

OBJECTIVE

"To enhance literacy rates in Rajasthan's villages by 25% within five years."

Why join us...

Your Ultimate Launchpad to Academic and Professional Success

In today’s fast-paced world, choosing the right educational and professional path is key to unlocking a world of opportunities. Whether you are a student at the brink of higher education or a professional aiming to scale new heights, our platform is designed to provide you with a seamless, all-in-one learning experience. Read on to discover how we can be the catalyst for your success!

Empowering High School Students: XI and XII for Arts and Commerce

The foundation of a successful career begins in high school. Our dedicated programs for XI and XII students, specifically tailored for Arts and Commerce streams, focus on:

In-depth Subject Knowledge:
We ensure that students grasp the fundamentals as well as advanced concepts, preparing them for competitive entrance exams and future academic challenges.
Skill Development:
Beyond textbooks, our curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, effective communication, and problem-solving skills.
Personalized Guidance:
Expert mentors provide one-on-one counseling to help students choose the best paths for their higher education and career aspirations.

By providing a structured yet dynamic learning environment, we aim to empower young minds to confidently transition from school to higher education.

Advancing University Studies: All Semesters for B.A. and B. Com

For undergraduate students pursuing Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Commerce degrees, our services offer a comprehensive academic support system:

Semester-Wise Guidance:
Whether you are starting your first semester or wrapping up your final year, our structured programs cover every stage of your academic journey.
Interactive Learning:
With access to expert tutorials, interactive sessions, and group discussions, students can reinforce their understanding of complex subjects.
Career-Centric Approach:
We help bridge the gap between academic theories and real-world applications, preparing students for the evolving demands of the job market.

Our commitment is to not only support your academic growth but also to prepare you for a thriving career post-graduation.

Diving into Professional Courses: CA, CS, and CWA

Professional courses like Chartered Accountancy (CA), Company Secretary (CS), and Cost and Work Accountancy (CWA) are gateways to some of the most respected careers in the business world. Our specialized training programs offer:

Expert-Led Instruction:
Learn from industry experts who bring practical insights and years of experience to the classroom.
Rigorous Exam Preparation:
Our intensive coaching and tailored study materials are designed to help you clear even the toughest examinations with confidence.
Real-World Exposure:
With case studies, internships, and live project sessions, we ensure that you gain not only theoretical knowledge but also practical skills essential for professional success.

These courses are meticulously structured to guide you through every challenge, ensuring that you emerge as a competent and confident professional ready to tackle the corporate world.

Enhancing Communication: Spoken English and Personality Development

In today’s globalized environment, effective communication and a strong personality are as important as academic credentials. Our Spoken English and Personality Development programs are designed for students and professionals across all streams:

Fluency and Confidence:
Improve your spoken English through interactive sessions, real-life conversations, and engaging practice sessions.
Holistic Development:
Our programs focus on grooming your personality, enhancing your body language, and developing a confident demeanor that leaves a lasting impression.
Versatile Curriculum:
Whether you’re preparing for interviews, presentations, or simply aiming to improve daily communication, our courses are tailored to meet diverse needs.

By refining your communication skills and boosting your confidence, we prepare you to excel in any professional setting and to express yourself with clarity and impact.
