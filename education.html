WHAT IS EDUCATION

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes through learning, experience, or teaching. It involves the development of intellectual, emotional, and social abilities that help individuals understand the world, make informed decisions, and contribute to society. Education can take place in formal settings like schools and universities, or through informal learning experiences throughout life.

EDUCATION THROUGH GEETASAAR

Through GeetaSaar, we will be over coming academic gaps and encourage holistic development, and foresee long-term transformation.
